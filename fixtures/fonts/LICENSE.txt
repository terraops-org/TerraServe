DejaVuSans.ttf — DejaVu Fonts, a permissive (Bitstream Vera + DejaVu changes)
redistributable license. Committed as the pinned font for deterministic label
rendering (spec §8). Latin coverage suffices for the MVP; a Noto Sans (SIL OFL)
can replace it at the i18n/fallback rung for broader script reach.
Upstream: https://dejavu-fonts.github.io/
